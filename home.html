About Us

That Avadhoot Packs converts paper into boxes is a fact. Indeed, box making is at the heart of what we do - but it still doesn’t tell our entire story.

At Avadhoot, we believe that the humble brown boxes we make deliver hope and happiness to millions. We understand our responsibility in safeguarding your hope and happiness. And we take it very seriously.

Our packages carry a variety of products to the last mile from your child’s favourite chips to the cooking oil that keeps your heart healthy, to your favourite brand of whisky and of course your family’s most awaited e-commerce package.

We have been in the business of box making since 1963. Building on our experience, we scaled up to our first Automatic operations at Ranjangaon near Pune, Maharashtra in 2005. We started our second Automatic Operations at a bigger scale at Bagnan, near Kolkata, West Bengal in 2013.

At the heart of all our solutions, lies our core philosophy that at delivery, client’s product will only be as good as our packaging

—Laksh
