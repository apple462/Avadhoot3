About Us

It is known that Avadhoot Packs converts paper into boxes. Indeed box making is at the heart of what we do- but that hardly tells our complete story.

At Avadhoot, we believe that the humble brown boxes we make deliver hope and happiness to millions. We understand our responsibility in safeguarding your hope and happiness. And we take it very seriously.

Our packages carry a variety of products to the last mile from your child’s favourite chips to the cooking oil that keeps your heart healthy, to your favourite brand of whisky and of course your family’s most awaited e-commerce package.

We have been in the business of box making since 1963. Building on our experience, we scaled up to our first Automatic operations at Ranjangaon near Pune, Maharashtra in 2005. We started our second Automatic Operations at a bigger scale at Bagnan, near Kolkata, West Bengal in 2013. The Food & Beverages sector has been our focus right from the inception, upholding us to the highest industry standards and best practices in packaging. Beyond the F&B sector, we have successfully expanded our fold to FMCG, e-commerce, textile, liquor, modern retail among other sectors. We have a deep understanding of the quality & service needs of our MNC as well as regional clients from diverse industries.

Today, we are one of the leading suppliers of corrugated boards, boxes and related accessories to our esteemed clientele including 15+ MNCs and Fortune 500 companies. We derive our delight from a continued association with satisfied customers since the initiation of our relationships. With our Head Office in Mumbai and manufacturing units in East and West India, we offer an eclectic mix of the regional to multi-location capabilities to our clients.
